FAQ

Clear boundaries to keep expectations realistic.

Do you provide compliance certification (HIPAA/PCI/etc.)?

No. I implement technical best practices and provide documentation. For formal compliance attestation, you should work with a qualified compliance professional/auditor.

Do you offer penetration testing?

Not as a service. If you need a formal penetration test, I can help you prepare and refer you to a qualified provider.

What do you need from me to start?

A list of devices, who manages your email/accounts, and admin access where required. If you don’t know, that’s normal—I'll walk you through it.

Do you do on-site work?

Yes, for Des Moines metro clients. Remote support is also available depending on the scope.
