FAQ

Clear boundaries to keep expectations realistic.

Do you provide compliance certification (HIPAA/PCI/etc.)?

No. Services focus on implementing technical best practices and providing documentation. Formal compliance attestation should be completed by a licensed auditor or compliance professional.

Do you offer penetration testing?

No. If a formal penetration test is required, I can help you prepare your environment and coordinate with a qualified third-party provider.

What do you need from me to start?

A list of devices, who manages your email/accounts, and admin access where required. If you don’t know, that’s normal—I’ll walk you through it.

Do you provide on-site support for homes and small offices?

Yes. On-site support is available for homes and small offices in the Des Moines metro area. Remote support is also available depending on the service.
